Chiles Rellenos Souffle

You’ll only need a few ingredients—avocado, eggs, feta, seasoning, a tortilla and lime—to make this TikTok egg breakfast. Start by heating a pan on the stove, then sprinkle feta cheese around the edges and crack an egg in the middle. Cook the egg as you typically would for a fried egg, then serve it over a tortilla with smashed avocado. It’s so quick and easy to make, you’ll definitely want to add it to your breakfast rotation.

Ingredients

  • 2 cans (4 ounces each) chopped green chiles
  • 1/4 cup sliced ripe olives
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 cups sharp shredded cheddar cheese
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1-1/2 cups biscuit/baking mix
  • 2 cups 2% milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 cup 4% small-curd cottage cheese
  • Optional: Salsa and sour cream

Directions

Step 1:

Preheat oven to 350°. Spread green chiles in a greased 11x7-in. baking dish; sprinkle with olives, onion and cheese. In a large bowl, whisk eggs, biscuit mix, milk and pepper until blended. Stir in cottage cheese; pour over top.

Step 2:

Bake, uncovered, until golden brown, puffed and a knife inserted in the center comes out clean, 45-50 minutes. Let stand 5-10 minutes before serving. If desired, serve with salsa and sour cream.

Step 3:

To Make Ahead: Refrigerate unbaked souffle, covered, several hours or overnight. To use, preheat oven to 350°. Remove souffle from refrigerator while oven heats. Bake, as directed, increasing time as necessary until golden and puffed, and a knife inserted in the center comes out clean. Let stand 5-10 minutes before serving.
