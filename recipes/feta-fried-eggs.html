Feta Fried Eggs

You’ll only need a few ingredients—avocado, eggs, feta, seasoning, a tortilla and lime—to make this TikTok egg breakfast. Start by heating a pan on the stove, then sprinkle feta cheese around the edges and crack an egg in the middle. Cook the egg as you typically would for a fried egg, then serve it over a tortilla with smashed avocado. It’s so quick and easy to make, you’ll definitely want to add it to your breakfast rotation.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons feta cheese, crumbled
  • Pinch of ground black pepper and crushed red chili flakes, optional
  • Tortillas, avocado and lime juice, for serving

Directions

Step 1: Fry the egg with feta

Heat a small nonstick skillet over medium heat. Once hot, sprinkle the feta around the edge of a pan, leaving the center free of cheese. Let the cheese melt for a few seconds, then crack the egg into the center of the skillet. Season with pepper and chili flakes, if desired. Cook for 1-2 minutes until the white turns opaque, then cover the skillet with a lid.

Continue to cook until the whites are fully set, and some white has set over the yolk, about 1 to 2 minutes.

Step 2: Serve as desired

Uncover, let any excess water in the skillet cook off for 30 seconds and then serve the feta egg as desired. One popular TikTok method from @graceelkus is serving the finished feta egg over a tortilla with smashed avocado and a squeeze of fresh lime juice.

Editor’s Tip: Allow the excess water in the skillet to evaporate after uncovering the egg. That’s the trick to getting those perfect crispy feta cheese edges!
